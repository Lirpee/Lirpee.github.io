Dragon

Dragon is a Beast-type Blox Fruit that costs 3,500,000 from the Blox Fruit Dealer

This is the first Beast type Blox Fruit that is Mythical tier. It is also the fourth Beast type fruit (first three being Buddha, Falcon and Phoenix with the fifth being Leopard).

This Blox Fruit allows its user to enhance its attacks in transformed mode, similar to Phoenix v2, although its attacks are available to the player, even when not transformed, unlike Buddha and Falcon

This fruit is one among many others that have an animation on the physical form, the others being Rubber, Spider, Phoenix, Portal, Gravity, Dough, Venom, Control, Spirit and Leopard.

This fruit has no awakening.

Moveset

Passive

Fury

The user starts off with 50% of the "Fury Meter" bar when spawned. The user's Fury Meter needs to be full to be able to transform into a dragon. Fury is regained over time while not transformed and drains while transformed. Using moves while transformed decreases the user's Fury Meter. When taking damage while transformed, the Fury Meter will increase. The fury meter takes 1m 30s to fill to full from 0%.

Transformation

In Dragon form, the user gains a 62.5% damage resistance to all attacks.

Multicolored

The user can change the color of their wings and Dragon form by flying through colored hoops. However, various mastery levels are needed.

Green, blue, and red are unlocked at 0 mastery.

Orange is unlocked at 100 mastery.

Orange is unlocked at 100 mastery.

Brown is unlocked at 300 mastery.

Yellow is unlocked at 400 mastery.

White is unlocked at 500 mastery.

Black is unlocked at 600 mastery.
